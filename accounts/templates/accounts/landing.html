About Adonai Bible School

Equipping God's People
for Works of Service

We are committed to providing foundational theological education that equips the next generation for church leadership, church growth, and church planting.

Head — Intellectual Formation

Each module is biblically grounded in systematic theology, biblical studies, and church history — building a solid intellectual foundation in the Word of God.

Hands — Ministry Formation

Real-world ministry insights are integrated into every module, preparing students for active service in evangelism, pastoral care, discipleship, and church planting.

Heart — Spiritual Formation

Our learning program places importance on a growing relationship of loving obedience to Jesus Christ — nurturing the inner life of every student.

Church-Based Learning

Rooted in Adonai Church, Bangalore — a vibrant community of believers. Our training is shaped by and for the local church, not detached from it.

3 Quarters · 18 Modules

The academic year runs June through March across three quarters (Q1, Q2, Q3), with 6 modules per quarter — structured to fit alongside ministry commitments.

Experienced Faculty

Learn from pastors, theologians, and ministry leaders at Adonai Church who bring both academic depth and real-world ministry experience to every session.

Enrollment Options

Our Programs

Three tracks designed for believers at every stage — whether you're deepening your faith, earning a certificate, or pursuing individual modules.

Advanced

Graduate Diploma in Ministry

Recognized as equivalent to the first year of a Master's-level degree. Awarded upon full attendance across all 18 modules, successful completion of all assignments and module tests, and fulfilment of practical ministry requirements in Spiritual Formation, Discipleship, and Pastoral Care & Counselling.

Requirements
  • Full attendance — all 18 modules
  • All assignments & module tests
  • Spiritual Formation (KVL)
  • Discipleship (BGF)
  • Pastoral Care & Counselling (WFC I & II)
Duration: 1 Academic Year
Award: Graduate Diploma
Foundation

Certificate of Achievement in Ministry

Awarded based on full attendance at all 18 modules across the core disciplines of Theology, Old Testament, New Testament, Mission, and Leadership. An excellent foundation for any believer desiring to grow in Scripture and service.

Core Disciplines
  • Theology
  • Old Testament
  • New Testament
  • Mission
  • Leadership
Duration: 1 Academic Year
Award: Certificate
Flexible

Individual Course Enrollment

Designed for believers who wish to attend selected modules without committing to the full program. Choose specific modules aligned with your ministry interest or personal growth — at your own pace within the academic year.

Who This Is For
  • Believers exploring theological education
  • Those with specific ministry learning goals
  • Part-time or occasional participants
  • Church members wanting a focused topic
Duration: 1 Academic Year
Award: Course Completion

Our Mission

Raising Up Leaders
